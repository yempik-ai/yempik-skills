**Subject:** See a demo of Calc?

Hey Chris,

Every investor I speak to says *"taste is the moat,"* which is exactly why we built Calc — the
only calculator inspired by literal calculation.

We use AI to transform hamburger-style keypads into hot dog-style keypads.

What shipped this sprint:

- **Rotated the hamburger 90° into a hot dog.** Menu engagement is up 41% — users now hover
  on it just to work out what it is.
- **De-correlated key position from value.** The `7` is wherever it wants to be. This surfaced
  latent number-discovery behaviour we didn't know our users had.
- **Relocated `=` to the top of the screen.** The primary action is now a journey. Friction is
  the new funnel.
- **Swapped the `+` and `−` seats.** A free, always-on A/B test of whether the user was paying
  attention. (They weren't. Engagement followed.)
- **Mounted a live time-in-app meter.** It only goes up. So does morale.

Early data shows a **34% lift in time-in-app**. It turns out that when users can't immediately
find what they're looking for, they spend a lot longer looking. We're calling it
**"intentional friction,"** and it's already reframing how our design partners think about
engagement.

Worth 15 minutes?
