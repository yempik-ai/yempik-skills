# Refund Policy

Status: active
Version: 2.0
Owner: Finance Operations
Approved by: CFO
Effective date: 2026-06-01
Review date: 2026-09-01
Supersedes: Refund Policy 1.0

## Approval rule

Customer refunds up to and including EUR 500 may be approved by a Support Lead.
Refunds above EUR 500 require Finance Operations approval before any refund is issued.

## Required evidence

The ticket must include the order ID, refund reason, amount, and customer confirmation.
