# Decision: reduce the support refund threshold

Status: approved
Decision owner: CFO
Decision date: 2026-05-20
Effective date: 2026-06-01

The approval threshold for Support Leads changes from EUR 1,000 to EUR 500.
Finance Operations approval is required above EUR 500.

Reason: reduce uncontrolled refund exposure while the new fraud checks are introduced.

Follow-up: update the refund policy, the handling SOP, and the support agent instructions before the effective date.
