# Refund Handling SOP

Status: active
Version: 1.4
Owner: Customer Support
Last reviewed: 2026-02-10

## Procedure

1. Confirm the order ID and refund reason.
2. Record the requested amount in the support ticket.
3. A Support Lead may approve refunds up to and including EUR 1,000.
4. Escalate refunds above EUR 1,000 to Finance Operations.
5. Send the approved refund confirmation to the customer.

## Exceptions

Suspected fraud must always be escalated to Risk Operations.
