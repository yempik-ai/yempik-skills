# GTM Engineering Outbound Sample Prompts

## Should Trigger

- "Voglio costruire un outbound system per vendere servizi AI a PMI italiane. Parti da ICP e signal."
- "We use Clay and Smartlead but replies are weak. Audit our strategy, signals, copy, and delivery."
- "Ho una lista di startup italiane. Che enrichment e trigger devo creare prima di scrivere le email?"
- "Design a cold email campaign where each message starts from a real buying signal."
- "Il dominio sta peggiorando: come distinguo problema di deliverability da problema di lista/copy?"

## Should Not Trigger

- "Fammi un business plan completo per una startup food."
- "Crea un logo."
- "Traduci questo testo."
- "Riassumi questa registrazione."
- "Scrivi una funzione Python."

## Expected Output Qualities

- Names the bottleneck before listing tactics.
- Separates strategy, audience, signal, messaging, delivery, and metrics.
- Gives concrete signal examples with sources and caveats.
- Protects deliverability instead of pushing volume.
- Ends with first 72-hour actions.
