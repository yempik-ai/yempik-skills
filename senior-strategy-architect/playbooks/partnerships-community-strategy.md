# Partnerships and Community Strategy Playbook

Use for partner ecosystems, co-selling, reseller/referral/affiliate motions, integrations,
community-led growth, ambassador programs, creator collaborations, or member/customer communities.

## First diagnosis

Identify the real job:

- Access: reach customers you cannot efficiently reach alone.
- Trust: borrow credibility from partners/members/experts.
- Product value: integrations or ecosystem increase usefulness.
- Revenue: partner-sourced or partner-influenced deals.
- Retention: community/support/customer success improves outcomes.
- Advocacy: members/customers create proof and referrals.
- Feedback: community improves product insight.

Then find the constraint:

- Partner/member value is weak.
- Incentives are misaligned.
- No clear business objective.
- No repeatable ritual or motion.
- Too many low-quality partners/members.
- Measurement is vanity-based.
- Governance/moderation is missing.

## Partnership strategy choices

- Partner type: referral, reseller, affiliate, integration, co-sell, marketplace, strategic alliance, agency, influencer/creator.
- Mutual value: what each side gains.
- Target overlap: which accounts/audiences match.
- Motion: how opportunities are identified, activated, tracked, and closed.
- Incentives: commission, revenue share, leads, content, product access, status, enablement.
- Enablement: assets, training, scripts, co-marketing, deal registration.
- Metrics: sourced/influenced revenue, pipeline, activation, retention, attach rate.

## Community strategy choices

- Business objective: support, retention, education, advocacy, product feedback, acquisition, identity.
- Member identity: who belongs and why.
- Member value: learning, access, status, belonging, leads, tools, answers, recognition.
- Core behavior: what members repeatedly do.
- Rituals: events, challenges, office hours, AMAs, showcases, teardown sessions, peer reviews.
- Governance: rules, moderation, onboarding, contribution standards.
- Measurement: business + community health.

## Output must include

- Diagnosis.
- Partnership/community objective.
- Target partner/member profile.
- Value exchange.
- Operating motion or rituals.
- Enablement/governance.
- 30/60/90-day plan.
- Metrics and ROI logic.
- Risks and what not to do.

## Metrics

Partnerships:
- Partner-sourced pipeline/revenue.
- Partner-influenced pipeline/revenue.
- Activation rate by partner.
- Deal velocity/win rate with partners.
- Integration adoption.
- Revenue share/margin.
- Partner churn.

Community:
- Activated members, not total members.
- Contribution rate and repeat participation.
- Support deflection or time-to-answer.
- Product feedback quality.
- Retention/expansion among community participants.
- Advocacy/referrals.
- Content or UGC quality.

## High-upside moves

- Build a partner account map before launching partner marketing.
- Start with 5 high-fit partners rather than 100 inactive logos.
- Create a recurring community ritual that produces assets: teardown, challenge, benchmark, showcase.
- Give members status and utility, not just a place to chat.
- Tie community moments to product value, success, or pipeline.

## Failure modes

- Logo collection instead of active partnerships.
- Communities with no member job.
- Measuring members/posts without business linkage.
- Misaligned incentives.
- No owner for partner/community operations.
