# Sample Prompts to Test the Skill

Use these prompts after installing the skill. Good outputs should be specific, ask only necessary questions,
and avoid generic advice.

## 1. Vague marketing prompt

“Bro fammi una strategia marketing per vendere un servizio di AI automation alle PMI italiane. Budget 1000€/mese, sono da solo, niente fuffa.”

Expected behavior:
- Treat as marketing + GTM.
- Ask only missing high-value questions or proceed with assumptions.
- Produce a concrete wedge, message, channel plan, examples, and first 72 hours.

## 2. GTM launch

“We're launching a compliance SaaS for EU fintechs in 90 days. Build a GTM strategy with ICP, positioning, channels, sales motion, and metrics.”

Expected behavior:
- Research mode if current regulation/market facts are needed.
- GTM stage, beachhead, sales-led motion likely.
- Risk/proof and buyer committee included.

## 3. Pricing

“We sell a B2B analytics tool at €99/month per seat but customers say pricing is confusing and expansion is low. Suggest a new pricing strategy.”

Expected behavior:
- Diagnose value metric/packaging.
- Propose tiers, value metric tests, migration plan, metrics.

## 4. Product/growth

“Users sign up for our productivity app but only 8% come back after week one. What strategy should we use?”

Expected behavior:
- Diagnose activation/retention before acquisition.
- Define aha moment, onboarding experiments, retention metrics.

## 5. Competitive response

“A competitor just launched a cheaper version of our service. How should we respond without racing to the bottom?”

Expected behavior:
- Competitive posture, value proof, segmentation, pricing/packaging options, what not to do.

## 6. Fundraising narrative

“We have €18k MRR, 12% MoM growth, 4 enterprise pilots, and want to raise a €1M seed. Help with investor narrative.”

Expected behavior:
- Narrative spine, proof gaps, deck flow, objections, milestones, investor targeting.
